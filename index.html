Award-Winning Tech Entrepreneur

I'm Mercy Nekesa, a results-oriented Engineer with a proven track record in developing innovative solutions for complex business challenges. My experience in leading the development of Raining Vegetables, a data-driven platform serving over 7,000 farmers, demonstrates my ability to build scalable systems that deliver tangible business impact. Recognized for my work in technology and entrepreneurship in Belgium and Uganda, I am committed to partnering with organizations to drive growth through software solutions.

My Recent Work

Partnering with organizations & companies to develop cutting-edge software solutions that deliver tangible results.

Raining Vegetables

Transformed agricultural practices through Raining Vegetables, a data-driven platform empowering farmers with essential insights to boost yields and promote sustainable farming.

Sundetails Technologies

Streamlined operations for small businesses with Sundetails Technologies, a robust software solution that digitizes processes, improves efficiency, and drives growth.

Lecturer, Faculty of Engineering, Design and Technology, UCU (Uganda Christian University)

Cultivated future tech leaders through curriculum development at UCU, focusing on computer science research projects, software development & project management, and digital marketing to bridge the gap between academia and industry.

UICT, Uganda Institute of Information and Communications Technology

As a collaboration partner of UICT, I am involved in planning and execution of multiple high-impact initiatives, including the Agritech Hackathon, NASA Hackathon (Kampala), and several other undisclosed projects. My role encompasses providing expert advice and hands-on support throughout the project lifecycle, driving innovation and ensuring project deliverables are met.

NARO, National Agricultural Research Organisation

Collaborated closely with NARO doing comprehensive field research on agro-ecological and water management practices. Through extensive watershed visits and community engagement, I gained deep insights into local livelihoods and challenges. Our platform, Raining Vegetables is intended to support NARO's data collection efforts, contributing to the development of evidence-based strategies for sustainable and resilient agriculture.

ADC, Agribusiness Development Centre

In a strategic partnership with ADC, we're providing a suite of digital agricultural solutions to enhance their farmer support services. Our SMS weather alerts have proven to be a valuable tool in optimizing farming practices. Our collaboration further extends additional features from our platform, such as crop advisory services and market price information, to further empower ADC and their farmer communities.
