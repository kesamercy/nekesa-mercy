I've spent the last decade building software in East Africa, scaling agricultural platforms to over 7,000 farmers and partnering with national research institutions. Through that work, I learned that the hardest problems in software aren't technical—they're about aligning what people need with what's possible to build.

What I'm Building Now

Zorentia

Decision infrastructure for building software. We transform raw ideas into complete, executable sequences. Our Ship Plans provide the engineering logic that covers what most builds miss: the strategic trade-offs behind technical choices, the exact order of execution, the specific checks that prove your work is complete, and the troubleshooting resources when builds stall. The result is you ship a working product while learning how to build the next one faster.

Selected Work

Raining Vegetables

A data platform that provides farmers with agricultural guidance. Serves over 7,000 farmers across Uganda, with clients including National Agricultural Research Organisation (NARO) and Agribusiness Development Centre (ADC).

Daily Monitor UBC TV

Medical Translation Project

Developed an LSTM-based machine learning algorithm to translate medical forms between African languages. Built this to address the gap where Google Translate only supported 13 of 2,000+ African languages at the time, creating both the translation model and a medical translator application for doctor-patient communication.

Teaching at UCU

Developed curriculum and taught courses at Uganda Christian University's Faculty of Engineering, focusing on bridging the gap between academic computer science and real-world application.

Agricultural Research

Collaborated with the National Agricultural Research Organisation (NARO) on field research studying agro-ecological and water management practices through watershed visits and community engagement.

Sundetails Technologies

Software that helped small businesses digitize their operations. Built to solve the specific challenges I saw in local business processes.
